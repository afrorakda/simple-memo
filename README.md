# Simple Memo
The fastest, most minimal scratchpad that auto-saves every character to your browser.

## Philosophy
* **1 Tool, 1 Action.** No folders, no sync lag, just writing.
* **No Login / No Ads / No Tracking**
* **Browser-only Privacy** (Works perfectly on low-spec devices and mobile).

## How to Use
1. **Open & Type:** Keyboard focuses automatically. Start writing your thoughts instantly.
2. **Close & Return:** Your text is auto-saved. It will be there exactly as you left it when you return.
